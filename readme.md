# RM Website

WebApp for Bed & Breakfast Management.

Features:
- Landing Page
  - Showcase rooms
  - Showcase services
  - Policy
- User Registration
- Login/Logout
- Bookings
  - Rooms and services
  - Reviews
  - Payments
- User area
  - My Bookings
  - My Profile
  - My Payments
  - My Reviews
- Admin area
  - User managemnt
  - Payments management
  - Reviews management
  - Rooms management
  - Bookings management
  - Services management
  - Reports
  - Customer support
- Rooms management
- Payments
- Automatic payment validation and customer emailing confirmation
